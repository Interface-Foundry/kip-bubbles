The Team

Rachel Law CEO

A born entrepreneur, Rachel found early success building and designing Vortex, a browser extension that let people swap metadata. The product went viral and was featured in Ad-Age, Hacker News, and Slashdot.

JR Baldwin CTO

Award-winning UX Designer, JR has designed & developed advanced WiFi mesh networks, executed applications for the Red Hook Initiative and Open Technology Institute, and has lectured at the White House and Harvard University.

Ross Leonardy Lead Front-End Developer

Always creating and innovating, Ross was the former editor and developer for DIS Magazine. He’s since worked at various design companies in NYC and has been featured in New York Times for his projects.
